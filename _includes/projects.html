  Treatment Analysing Mortality Predictor (GE Hack) (Jan 2020)

  Microsoft- MMI Automata

Microsoft- MapMyIndia Automata is an automation pipeline that uses NLP and Computer Vision to automate the task of adding a location or reporting an incident on the map across India. This information is crowdsourced in the form of text and images, and the automata verify information using text processing and leveraging cloud vision APIs. The algorithm also implements a timestamp algorithm and geotagging to check the freshness of the image. This framework achieved the operational efficiency of multiple hours to seconds, while totally eliminating the human intervention which currently persists

Technologies: Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Python, SQL
